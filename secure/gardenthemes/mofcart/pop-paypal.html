More About PayPal

PayPal is one of the safest, and most progressive ways on the internet today for making payments online. There are over 30 million PayPal members World wide, with over 42,000 online shops accepting PayPal as a payment method. PayPal is the leading payment processor for internet based auctions, and holds countless awards for eCommerce innovations. PayPal uses state of the art security, not only with encryption technologies, but with innovative Fraud Protection measures, a Merchant verification system, buyers security policies, FDIC insured.

PayPal is very easy to use. When you click on our PayPal button to complete your purchase, you will be taken to the secure PayPal system site. If you are not a current PayPal member, then you will be able to set up a PayPal membership, which will allow you to finalize your purchases. The PayPal membership is completely FREE for consumers.

PayPal members can complete payments in a few clicks, much faster than entering your billing information and credit card numbers for traditional online payments. It's also safer than traditional credit card payments over the internet.

If you are already a PayPal member, then you know how easy it is to complete your purchase from our PayPal button. You will be taken to the secure PayPal system site, where you can finalize your payment with a few clicks.

For more information about PayPal, visit the PayPal site - PayPal.com. They have an abundance of information on internet shopping and security.
