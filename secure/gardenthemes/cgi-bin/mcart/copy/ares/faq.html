Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I change my Account Information?
A. Click on the "Account Log In" tab above, then when you log-in, make sure you dot "Change Registration Information" first.

Q. When do I receive payment?
A. Referral revenue is paid to you monthly by the 10th of the following month. Accounts with balance less than $25.00 will be carried over to the following pay period.

Q. What do I make for revenue?
A. Revenue will be credited to you upon payment approval from the customer. 10% of sales made from your Affiliate link will be paid to you. The customer will receive a 10% discount on all orders placed.

Q. What kind of cash can I make?
A. Revenue can increase very quickly, depending on how many you refer. You can almost make about $3.00 on each order. For an example, if you refer just 25 people who purchase our product for $29.95 you'll receive a $75.00 dollar credit on your account. This can be pretty easy if you have a news letter.

Q. Where can I use my referring links?
A. Almost anywhere except SPAM. Websites, newsletters, mailing lists, School Magazines, newspapers and much more.
