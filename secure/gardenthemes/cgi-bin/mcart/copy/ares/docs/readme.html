ARES is a powerful tool to assist you in promoting your products online. It is not a stand alone program, but was written as a sister program to the Merchant OrderForm - Shopping Cart programs ver 1.54 and up. I have included it in the MOFcart v2.5 installation

ARES is a two tier Representative, Affiliate earnings system that uses "Affiliate Links" to track referrals to your web site and products. The ARES scripts manage the Affiliate Earnings system, and the Merchant OrderForm Shopping Cart will utilize the ARES logs to credit any Affiliates that are found at the final checkout process.

With ARES and Merchant OrderForm Shopping Cart you can:

  • Give customers sent to you via Affiliate Links discounts on their purchases
  • Credit an Affiliate commissions each time a purchase is made originating from them
  • Credit overall Representatives with first tier commissions on Affiliate credits

The system is administered from your web browser, once installed you can:

  • Set rates of discounts for customers
  • Set rates of Affiliate or Rep earnings
  • View pending or historical earnings for Reps, Affiliates
  • Create invoices for payment of pending earnings
  • Create stats on Affiliates activity
  • Generate mailing lists of Customers, Affiliates, Reps
  • Search on any field for Affiliates
  • Assign specific redirection urls for Affiliates
  • Void transactions
  • Edit Affiliate or Rep personal information
  • Pop up email from everywhere
