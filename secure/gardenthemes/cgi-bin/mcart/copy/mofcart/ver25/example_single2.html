Usage  
   
You offer items for sale on various pages on your site. Your customers add those items to their "basket" from any page as they browse through your site.
  
Instructions: Click the Buy button next to this item to add it to your cart.
 

Example  
   
Wooden Buddha $ 129.00

Handcrafted wooden Buddha made in Thailand
 

Syntax  
   

<FORM method=POST action="http://www.gardenthemes.com/cgi-bin/mcart/mof.cgi">
<INPUT type="hidden" name="postmode" value="SINGLEPOST">
<INPUT type="hidden" name="quantity" value="1">

<INPUT type="hidden" name="order"
value="Wooden Buddha----[b]Handcrafted Buddha[/b]: Made in Thailand----129.00----1----0">

<input type="image" src="buynow.gif" width=25 height=36 border=0
alt="Click Here to Add To Cart">
</FORM>

 

Notes  
   

This submits one product at a time to the cart by clicking on the buy button

The main product is submitted as hidden input. Also, note the use of HTML [ pseudo tags ] for sending the image to the cart

If you wanted to add product options to this see: example_options1.html
