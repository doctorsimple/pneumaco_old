Usage  
   

You offer items that fit nicely on a simple, one-page order form and do not require quantity, size, color, or other options.

Instructions: Check the items you wish to purchase, and click the add to cart button. Those items will be added to your basket.

 

Example  
   

Price Our List of Book, Videos, and Tapes
 $ 19.95  The Road To The White House, by Bill Gates, 2004  
 $ 29.95  Artificial Intelligence For Dummies, by Eye Que, 2004 
 $ 35.00  101 Great Silent Meditations, (12) Tape Audio Set  
 $ 359.95  How To Succeed On The Internet, (8) Video Tapes  
 $ 29.95  Searching For The Right Stuff, Internet Dating 101  
 

Syntax  
   

<FORM method=POST action="http://www.gardenthemes.com/cgi-bin/mcart/mof.cgi">
<INPUT type="hidden" name="postmode" value="CHECKBOXES">

<INPUT type=checkbox name="order"
value="1----The Road To The White House----[b]An upcoming book by Bill Gates[/b][br]The Road To The White House[br]A behind the sceens look at Microsoft and the man behind the legend----19.95----1----1">

<input type=checkbox name="order"
value="1----Artificial Intelligence For Dummies----[b]A book by Dr. Eye Que, III[/b][br]Dr. Que presents a no-nonsense approach for understanding the mysteries of Artificial Intelligence----29.95----1----1" >

<input type="image" src="addtocart2.gif" width=98 height=19 border=0
alt="Click Here to Add To Cart">
</FORM>
 

Notes  
   

This sets up rows of checkbox input using CHECKBOXES mode.

Note the order data is simply submitted as the value of the checkbox. When the add to cart button is clicked, all checked items are added to the cart

No product options are allowed for this type input

Note that quantity is submitted as the first field placeholder in the order input
value="Qty----Item----Desc----Price----ShipCode----TaxCode"
