Notes

This submits a product with a base price, and seven product options. Four product options are required, and three are add ons (not required input)

Note the main product "Select Print Size" is chosen from a dropbox of options, name=order
We have defaulted the Print Size to Print Size: 9.5 x 12 $ 99.00

All seven product option field names must be defined in <mof.conf>

  • First in %product_fields
  • And (if required) in @field_validation

Six fields (Material, Frame, Matte, Glass, Hardware, Insurance ) are also price adjusting field names and must be defined in @field_adjustments

Note the special format for price adjusting fields: value="Description-----0.00"

We have also pre-selected No Frame, No Matte, and Heavy Paper as default selections in three of the required options. This is used instead of "" (null). A (null) would force the customer to make a selection. The method above has already made their default selection.

The three checkboxes are add ons, price adjusting, but not required

Note also the use of HTML [ pseudo tags ] for bold and line break formatting
