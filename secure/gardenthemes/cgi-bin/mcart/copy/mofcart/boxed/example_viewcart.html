Use: You need to provide a way for customers to get back to the Cart without actually having to place other items in the cart. You can call it a "ViewCart" or "Chackout".

Note: The ?viewcart function always links into the View Cart Screen. You cannot link into any other area of the cart such as Shipping or Billing Info from outside the cart. The ?viewcart link is simply to provide a link back into the cart once your customer has left their cart. They can only Checkout and complete the order by proceeding through the cart checkout sequence.

How it works: Provide an HREF link using the QueryString command ?viewcart appended to the URL for <mof.cgi>
