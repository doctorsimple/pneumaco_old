Plant It Herbs is an organic Chinese medicinal plant nursery, specializing in the cultivation of Chinese medicinal plants since 1996. All seed and plant stock are of the highest quality and have been authenticated to insure the correct material is being grown.

Growing in the display gardens and greenhouses are perennials, annuals, vines, shrubs and trees used in Chinese medicine for thousands of years. Over 250 species of Chinese medicinal plants grow in our southeastern Ohio gardens.

We offer internships, educational support and research initiatives. Garden tours and workshop presentations are available. Please contact us for more information.

Fresh and dried Chinese herbs from our gardens are available for the first time this season. We also grow custom orders for fresh or dried plant material.
