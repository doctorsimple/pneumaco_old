Shipping Information

Plants are shipped by UPS and the US Post Office. Please provide a street address for UPS, they do not deliver to PO Boxes. Ground service is recommended for the following states only: OH, IN, KY Western PA, WV, TN and lower MI. All other states please use 3 Day select or Priority Mail. AL and HI must use 2nd Day Air. All orders outside the U.S., please inquire. Plants are shipped April through November. Please inquire if you wish to receive plants at other times of the year.

Payment terms

A minimum order of $25.00 plus shipping is required. Checks or money orders are accepted. At this time I do not accept credit cards. Sorry. Your checks will be deposited as soon as your order is processed. This assures inventory is reserved for you. Sorry no COD's.

Shipping costs

Shipping charges reflect what it cost us to get our plants to you, no handling fees are charged and are subject to change.

UPS Groundtrak and US Post Office - $9.00 plus $1.00 a plant

2nd Day Air and International Orders please inquire about shipping costs.

How to Use the Shopping Cart

*Directly below each item in the catalog there are two buttons:"add to shopping cart" and "go to order form". Click the "add" button once for each item you wish to order. Click the "go to order form" button when you have made all your selections, and you will be sent to the secured order form page, where you can enter a quantity for each item and calculate your total cost. Note that if you have javascript turned off in your browser, you will have to note the stock number, plant name and price and enter it in the order form manually.
